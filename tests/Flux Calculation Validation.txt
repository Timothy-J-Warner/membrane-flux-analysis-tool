## Validation of Output

Surface area of the membrane 	= 2 * pi * fiber radius * fiber length * number of fibers
				= 2 * pi * 1.2 / 2 / 1000 * 10.0 / 100 * 3
				= 0.001131 (m^2)
Test Duration 			= 60 (s) = 0.016667 (hr)
Flux 				= Mass change (kg) / Surface area (m^2) / Test Duration (hr)
